# Project Sample: Solar PV Electrical Documentation

## Scope

Prepare electrical documentation support for solar PV installation.

## Inputs

- PV module datasheet
- Inverter datasheet
- Site layout
- Cable route plan
- Earthing requirement
- Protection equipment list

## Deliverables

- Preliminary BOM
- Cable schedule
- Combiner/inverter notes
- Earthing and lightning notes
- Commissioning checklist

## Engineering Checks

- DC cable routing
- AC connection
- Earthing continuity
- Surge protection
- Mechanical protection
- Labeling and safety signs
